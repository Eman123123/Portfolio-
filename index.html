CycleGAN Unpaired Image Translation

Implemented CycleGAN from scratch in PyTorch for Sketch-Photo translation without paired training data. Used ResNet generators with PatchGAN discriminators, applying cycle consistency (λ=10) and identity loss (λ=5). Trained with mixed precision on dual T4 GPUs and evaluated using SSIM & PSNR metrics for image quality assessment.
